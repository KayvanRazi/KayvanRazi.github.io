About

I'm an Iranian computational biologist and PhD candidate currently based in East Lansing, Michigan, where I work as a Visiting Scholar at Michigan State University in collaboration with Dr. Cedric Gondro.

My work focuses on applying deep learning models to genomic prediction problems — asking how we can better understand the hidden genetic architecture behind complex biological traits.

I'm open to research collaborations, postdoctoral opportunities, and data science roles in computational biology or ML-driven life sciences.
